# Machine Learning Group

We are a group of computer science researchers who cooperate with different institutions in order to develop state-of-the-art research in machine learning. Some of our main research directions are: deep learning, fairness and recommendation systems

## Featured Projects

**Fairness**
With the widespread use of AI systems in our society, governed by laws, it is important to develop algorithms that are not discriminatory for certain groups in society. Here, we develop algorithms that minimize the factors that lead to unfair decision-making for a given previously informed group. Additionally, we have developed a framework for Fairness in Python language in order to add value to the community.  

**Recommender Systems**

Here we develop algorithms for robust recommendation systems for noise and attacks, as well as new forms of data representation. We also develop public frameworks in order to contribute to the community. in particular, the Persian framework, coded in Julia language.


## Team

This group is composed of undergraduate students of computer science at UFRRJ and students of computer science at the postgraduate level of PESC/COPPE. Despite the composition in different physical places, our team is in constant cooperation.
